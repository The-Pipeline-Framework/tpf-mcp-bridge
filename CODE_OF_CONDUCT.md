# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- using welcoming and inclusive language
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- focusing on what is best for the community
- showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- public or private harassment
- publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report issues to [team@pipelineframework.org](mailto:team@pipelineframework.org). Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct.
